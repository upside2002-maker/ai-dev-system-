## Scope of iter-5 (single fix)

Owner verbatim feedback after iter-4 (`83f548f`):
> сатурн снова плохо он вообще не похож!

**Diagnosis.** Iter-4 hook used three chained quadratic curves
(`Q 6 8 6 11 Q 6 13 3 12 Q 5 11 5 9`) which loop back on themselves.
At `stroke-width=2.4` the overlapping curves render as a small closed
"blob" instead of the canonical open sickle curve, so the eye no longer
reads ♄.

**Fix.** Replaced the hook with a single quadratic forming a clean
quarter-circle:

```
M 0 -10 L 0 7    # vertical stem
M -5 -7 L 5 -7   # T-bar (3 units below top → stem extends above)
M 0 7 Q 5 7 5 11 # single-quadratic open hook to right-down
```

This is the **simple primary** path landed — Wikipedia fallback was not
needed (standalone self-inspection PNG showed canonical ♄ on first try).
